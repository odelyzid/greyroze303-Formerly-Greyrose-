If you successfully reached the Wizard101 "Test OK" page in your normal web browser, but have arrived at this page during the second part of the test, there is probably firewall or internet security software on your computer that is blocking Wizard101.

1) Please verify that your firewall allows both WizardLauncher.exe and WizardGraphicalClient.exe to access the internet. If your software asks for specific port numbers, please allow TCP and UDP ports 12000 through 12999.

WizardLauncher.exe is located under the Wizard101 installation directory:
C:\ProgramData\KingsIsle Entertainment\Wizard101\PatchClient\BankA\
There may be another WizardLauncher.exe to allow at \PatchClient\BankB\

WizardGraphicalClient.exe is located under the Wizard101 installation directory:
C:\ProgramData\KingsIsle Entertainment\Wizard101\Bin

2) If allowing these files and ports does not work, you can also try temporarily disabling your firewall to see if this allows you access to Wizard101.

If your internet connection is working properly and you experience continued problems, please tell us that you have error code FW and click here for technical support.
